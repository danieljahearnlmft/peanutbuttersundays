Terms of Use & Privacy

How to use this website and our chat assistant, Jelly, and how we handle information.

Effective date: 6/21/26

About this page

Peanut Butter Sundays is a youth-founded 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Los Angeles. This page explains how to use this website and our chat assistant, "Jelly," and how we handle information.

About Jelly, our chat assistant

Jelly is an AI assistant. It can make mistakes and may give incomplete or inaccurate information. Its answers are for general information only and do not represent official statements or commitments by Peanut Butter Sundays.

Not professional or emergency help

Jelly does not provide medical, legal, financial, or mental-health advice, and it is not a crisis or emergency service. If you are in danger or experiencing an emergency, call 911. For mental-health or suicide crisis support, call or text 988. For local resources and services, dial 211.

How the chat works and your information

When you message Jelly, your messages are sent to a third-party AI provider (DeepSeek) to generate a reply. We do not store the content of your conversations, and your chat history exists only in your browser during your session. Please do not enter sensitive personal information in the chat, such as your full address, Social Security number, financial account details, or health information.

Privacy

We aim to collect as little personal information as possible through this site. If you contact us directly (for example, through the Contact page), we use your information only to respond to you. We do not sell your personal information.

Children

Our programs involve young people, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Please do not share personal details through the chat.

Changes

We may update this page from time to time. The effective date above shows when it was last changed.

Contact

Questions about this page? Reach us at info@peanutbuttersundays.com
