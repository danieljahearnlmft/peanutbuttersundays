The PBS Impact Fund

Youth Grants & Scholarships

The PBS Impact Fund puts resources directly into the hands of young people building community. We offer two grant types for youth-led projects and education.

Micro Grants Up to $1K

For youth-led community projects that need seed funding to get started.

Impact Scholarships Up to $5K

For young people pursuing education or training in community service and leadership.

Who can apply?

  • ✓ Youth ages 14-24 in Los Angeles
  • ✓ Community-focused projects or education goals
  • ✓ No prior nonprofit experience required
